🌿 About Campus Farm Online Store

We are a farm-fresh produce delivery service serving the University of Zambia (UNZA) Main Campus community. Our mission is to make fresh, affordable eggs and vegetables accessible to students, staff, and the wider campus family — with zero delivery charges.

🚚 Delivery Information

Delivery is completely FREE across UNZA Main Campus. Orders placed before 21:30 are delivered same day in the first 30 minutes of each hour (09:00–09:30, 10:00–10:30, etc.). Orders placed after 21:30 are delivered next morning at 09:00–09:30.

📞 Contact Us

For inquiries, bulk orders, or special requests, reach us via WhatsApp. We're happy to help!
