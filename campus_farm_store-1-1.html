🌿 About Campus Farm Online Store

We are a farm-fresh produce delivery service serving the University of Zambia (UNZA) Main Campus community. Our mission is to make fresh, affordable eggs and vegetables accessible to students, staff, and the wider campus family — with zero delivery charges.

🚚 Delivery Information

Delivery is completely FREE across UNZA Main Campus. Simply place your order, tell us your location on campus, and we'll bring it to you. Delivery is typically same-day or next morning depending on order time.

📞 Contact Us

For inquiries, bulk orders, or special requests, reach us via WhatsApp using the button at checkout. We're happy to help!
